About Me

I'm Neo. My research interest is in AI/ML and deep learning field. I have authored three papers (two of which I worked with a PhD student and one completely performed on my own) regarding applying ML/DL to design a predictive model to analyze EEG signals.

On a side note, since 2019, I've been deeply involved in competitive programming, representing Malaysia at the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI 2023). I'm currently also contributing as a problem setter and as a national trainer for the Malaysian Informatics and Programming Society.
